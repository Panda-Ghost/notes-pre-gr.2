&\cos (\alpha + \beta )=\sin \alpha + \sin \beta \\ \iff 
&\cos \alpha \cos \beta-\sin \alpha \sin \beta=\sin \alpha + \sin \beta \\ \iff
&\sin \alpha = (1+\sin \alpha)\sin \beta + (-\cos \alpha) \cos \beta \\
\end{aligned}
$$
