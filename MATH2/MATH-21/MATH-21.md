# MATH-21

设 $f:(a,b)\to \mathbb{R}, \; n$ 次可导，$x_{0}\in(a,b)$

求多项式 $P:\mathrm{deg}\;P=n$

$$
\begin{aligned}
p^{(k)}(x_{0})=f^{(k)}(x_{0}),\quad k=0,1,2,\dots,\quad p^{(0)}=p, f^{(0)}=f
\end{aligned}
$$

待定系数

$$
\begin{aligned}
p(x)=c_{0}+c_{1}(x-x_{0})+c_{2}(x-x_{0})^{2}+\dots +c_{n}(x-x_{0})^{n} \\

\end{aligned}
$$
