#命题 

设 $a_{n}\in \mathbb{R},\;n=1,2,\dots,\; \sum a_{n}$ 收敛

设 $n_k\in \mathbb{N}^{*}, \; k=1,2,\dots, \; \forall k\in \mathbb{N}^{*},\;n_{k+1}>n_{k}$

令 

$$
\begin{aligned}
&b_{1}=a_{1}+a_{2}+\dots+a_{n_{1}}\\
&b_{2}=a_{n_{1}+1}+a_{n_{1}+2}+\dots+a_{n_{2}}\\
&\cdots
\end{aligned}
$$

则 $\sum b_{n}$ 收敛
